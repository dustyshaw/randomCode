Oven Roasted Chicken

  • Prep Time: 1 hour
  • Number of Servings:

Ingredients:

Mark as Owned Ingredient Quantitiy Expected Cost
Chives 1/2 Cup 1.25
Chicken Breasts 4 Breasts 10.43
Lemon Pepper Seasoning 2 Tbs 0.60

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 ℉.
  2. Pat chicken breasts dry with a paper towel.
  3. Use 1 tsp. of LemonPepper Seasoning and 1/2 tsp. papricka to season chicken breasts.
  4. Place chicken breasts on a oiled baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes or until internal temperatures have reached 165 ℉.
