Who Am I?

Friday, March 11, 2022

Do you remember those little toys that would try to guess what you were thinking? They were very popular when I was a little kid. It was just a little plastic ball with one screen to display text. There were a few buttons, but the most important was the Yes and the No button(if only life were that simple).

This toy fascinated me. I began to wonder how it worked, how did it always seem to guess what I was thinking? Even trying to trick it wouldn't work.

Well, while trying to plan this little website, I felt myself asking myself 20 questions: Who am I? What do I do? What do I want to say? Why do I need to say it this way?

To answer some of those questions: I am a Software Engineering student (not a very good one) and I am working towards a Bachelors degree. I wanted to make a blog to keep a record of what I learn in the off-chance that it might help someone else in their journey.

I am sure this website is going to grow and change as I get better at programming and as I learn more. I hope you stick around for the journey!
